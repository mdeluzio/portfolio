Look or Listen

Look or listen is an application that will take a subject of interest provided by the user and return relevant results in the form of both Youtube videos and podcasts. The Youtube videos can be played directly in the page or made full screen. The podcast results bring you to Listennotes.com in a new window which will provide a description and an option to play the podcast episode. This project was brought to life by HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and jQuery.
